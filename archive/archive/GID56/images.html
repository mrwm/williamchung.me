Penitencia Creek Park
 

Penitencia Creek Park is of the many Santa Clara County parks. With 38 acres of land, the park includes the Penitencia Creek and the Penitencia Pond.
    During my free time, I like to go running around my neighborhood and this is one of the many parks that I like to go running in. Whenever the weather is nice, park has a calming field of grass wrapping around a calm pond that is teeming with wildlife. In the past, there used to only be duckies and maybe a couple fishies here and there. But recently, I have been seeing some turtles appear here and there. After a bit of internet searching around, I found out that they were called red-eared sliders... Going off topic, red-eared sliders are considered to be a type of harmful invasive species. It's kinda vexing, but at the same time I don't really see the harm with the turtles in the pond. The only thing I can think of is the turtles competing for food with the fishies in the pond.
