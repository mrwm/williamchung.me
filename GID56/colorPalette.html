I am selling the 3D printer that got me into 3D printing. The Tronxy XY-100, also known as the STARTT clone, is an entry level 3D printer with a bed size of 120mm (X) × 140mm (Y) × 130mm (Z) print bed. This printer has support for popular plastic filaments of PLA and ABS, both at 1.75mm in diameter. Despite the entry level ranking of this 3D printer, it has an accuracy rating of ±0.1mm.
    I am also selling this printer along with some peripherals that came along with it when I bought it. The peripherals include two rolls of PLA filaments, one roll of partially used white and one roll of partially used black; a small bag of extra screws and bolts; a small set of hex wrenches; ×4 extra cables for the stepper motors; a couple spare 0.5/0.4/0.3mm nozzles; and anythything that you would like me to 3D model and print (as long as it is within the size of the printer).
