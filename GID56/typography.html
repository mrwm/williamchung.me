Blocked_
Blocked_ – a free and open-source typeface

This font was created for the final submission for the typography class over at Foothill College. Inspired by the font "Undefined Medium" by Andi Rueckel and "Cute Font" TypoDesign Lab. Inc, Blocked_ takes a look with parts of the glyph missing (in a corner or somewhere else). At the same time, the font aims to emulate a blocky look; hence, the unfinished name of Blocked_<font>.

After finishing the requirements for the final project, I decided to continue working on this font and try to complete it. As of right now, Blocked_ supports unicode characters ranging from U+0020 to U+007E. In simple terms, it's basically ABC... to XYZ, both upper and lowercase, and the punctuation marks on the ANSI keyboard layout.

Blocked_'s project page can be found on Github here. Otherwise, the font can be downloaded directly here.
