Photography

Hello, This is the page demonstrating the application of grids on HTML! Instead of talking about grid theory, I am going to be showcasing the pictures I take for fun. Some of the photos were taken when I go running and most of the other photos were taken when I am either hiking or walking around. I have never taken a photography class prior to this summer quarter (as I am currently enrolled in one now), so most of the photos here are what I deem to be 'interesting' or 'pretty good'.

Sadly, I did not have the money to get a decent dedicated camera for me to use when I took these pictures. I also did not trust myself with expensive equipment that I was not familiar with. As a result, almost all of the photos here were taken through phone cameras. On the side note, almost all photos I take pertain to something with nature or scenery.

These three photos are arranged in the order with the most recent first. The first photo was taken in Alumn Rock park on April 20, 2018. The second photo was taken in Saratoga on April 9, 2017; and the last photo was taken in Alviso Park on July 4, 2016.
